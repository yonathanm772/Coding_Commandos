Sarah, The Spontaneous Saver: Scenario 1

Sarah decides she wants to save for a spring break trip. She opens her finance app and sets up a new "Travel Fund" savings goal, specifying her target amount and deadline. The app breaks down how much she needs to save weekly, and she sets up automatic transfers from her checking account to help her meet this goal.
