Sarah, The Spontaneous Saver: Scenario 2

Sarah finds herself spending a little more than usual on dining out. She receives a weekly notification from the app showing her spending in different categories and realizes that she exceeded her dining budget. This notification helps her become more mindful of her expenses, and she decides to cut back on eating out the following week.
