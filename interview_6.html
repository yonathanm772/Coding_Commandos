Introduction to Product

Q1: ["We are developing a finance app targeted at helping college students manage their money. Does this sound like something you would use? Why or why not."]

A: [Maybe, depends on functions built within the finance app.]

Current Financial Management

Q2: ["How do you currently manage your personal finances (e.g., budgeting, saving)?"]

A: [Not a ton of managing just watch what I spend.]

Q3: ["What tools or apps, if any, do you currently use for managing your finances?"]

A: [Bank account and Credit Card app.]

Q4: ["What challenges do you face when trying to manage your money?"]

A: [Spending too much going out with friends.]

Current Financial Management

Q5: ["What features would you find most helpful in a finance app?"]

A: [Breakdown of money into sections to know where money can be spent.]

Q6: ["How important is it for you to have features like automatic expense tracking, savings goals, or financial tips?"]

A: [Mid to low importance.]

Q7: ["Would you prefer an app that offers spending alerts or savings advice? Why?"]

A: [Spending alerts, gives awareness of spending.]

Budgeting Preferences

Q8: ["When budgeting, do you prefer to manually input data, or would you prefer automated tracking linked to your bank account?"]

A: [Automated tracking.]

Q9: ["How often would you want to review your budget and spending trends (e.g., daily, weekly, monthly)?"]

A: [Weekly.]

User Experience

Q10: ["What design or interface features make an app easy or difficult for you to use?"]

A: [Something that has smooth transitions and is easy to navigate.]

Q11: ["How important is simplicity in an app versus having more advanced financial tools?"]

A: [Doesn’t have to be simple just needs to easy to navigate doesn’t have everything right next to each other.]

User Experience

Q12: ["Would you be interested in receiving personalized financial recommendations based on your spending habits?"]

A: [Yes.]

Q13: ["How important is it for the app to help you set and track financial goals?"]

A: [Extremely important.]

Social and Educational Elements

Q14: ["Would you be interested in features like community challenges for saving or social sharing of financial goals?"]

A: [No.]

Q15: ["How much do you value educational resources like articles or tips on financial literacy within the app?"]

A: [Medium to high.]

Concerns

Q16: ["What concerns do you have when using finance apps (e.g., privacy, security, difficulty)?"]

A: [Privacy and difficulty of use.]

Q17: ["What would make you stop using a finance app?"]

A: [If it wasn’t easy to navigate or was hard to use.]

Notifications and Alerts:

Q18: ["How often would you like to receive notifications or alerts from a finance app? What types of alerts (e.g., spending, savings goals, budgeting limits)?"]

A: [No more than daily, budgeting limits or spending too much toward something I shouldn’t. Comprehensive report weekly.]

Final Thoughts

Q19: ["If you could create the perfect finance app, what key feature(s) would it have?"]

A: [The ability to section money based on bills being due and by when getting paid.]

Q20: ["Is there anything you wish existing finance apps offered that they don’t?"]

A: [I do not currently use or have ever used a finance.]
