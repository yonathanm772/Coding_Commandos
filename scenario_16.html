Jordan, The Social Spender: Scenario 1

Jordan is invited to several social events over the weekend, and he knows he tends to overspend on outings. He sets a weekend budget in the app and enables spending alerts. Throughout the weekend, the app sends him notifications as he gets close to his limit, helping him stay within his budget.
